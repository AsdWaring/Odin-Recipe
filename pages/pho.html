Ingredients

  • Broth:
    • Water
    • Pot
    • Beef Soup Bones
    • Ginger
    • Fish sauce to taste
  • For Soup:
    • Steak
    • Basil
    • Rice Noodles
    • Carrots
    • Bok Choy
    • Hot Sauce
    • Anything else delicious you may concider

Instructions

  1. Broth:
    1. Boil all the ingredients for the broth for atleast 4 hours (longer if you can)
    2. Sift the Bones and Ginger out of the broth
    3. Keep on stove boiling
  2. Soup Fixings:
    1. Place all ingredients in bowls
    2. Cut Steak very thin and have at room temperature
    3. Make rice noodles
    4. Cut all vegatbles thinly
  3. Place steak at the bottom of your bowl
  4. Pour boiling broth onto the steak until covered
  5. Add vegetables, basil and rice noodles and more broth
  6. Add hot sauce or more fish sauce if wanted
  7. Enjoy
