Ingredients

  • Dirt
  • Water
  • Sun
  • Fertalizer
  • Seeds:
    • Lettuce Seeds
    • Spinach Seeds
    • Radish Seeds
    • Carrot Seeds
    • Or any other vegetables you like!

Instructions

  1. Take seeds and put in dirt
  2. Water seeds/dirt regularily
  3. Make sure it gets sun
  4. Fertalize as needed
  5. When plants are ready to harvest:
    1. Harvest plants
    2. Wash plants
    3. Cut plants with knife
    4. Put plants in bowl
    5. Eat Plants
